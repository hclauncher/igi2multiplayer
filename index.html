The IGI-2 Multiplayer Revival

IGI-2 Multiplayer is the official community by Homecoming Gaming fans around the world. Our mission is to bring the excitement of Project IGI2: Covert Strike back to life with modern tools, updated maps, and secure online gameplay. Whether you’re an old-school IGI player or new to tactical stealth shooters, our platform lets you play, host, and explore IGI2 online like never before.

The HCLauncheR is our all-in-one tool designed to simplify IGI-2 Multiplayer access. It automatically connects you to the best IGI2 servers, downloads maps, manages mods, and keeps your game updated. With just one click, you can join your friends, host custom matches, and enjoy smooth gameplay without the hassle of manual configuration.

For admins and server hosts, HcCommanDeR provides a powerful dashboard to monitor players, send RCON commands, and manage in-game events in real time. Together with HcBan protection, Homecoming Gaming ensures a fair, stable, and crash-free multiplayer experience for everyone.

Our IGI2 community is active 24/7, featuring tournaments, modding discussions, and development updates. We also offer a growing collection of IGI2 maps and community-made content to expand your game experience. From stealth missions to competitive multiplayer rounds, there’s always something happening at Homecoming.

Join our Discord and Facebook community to stay updated on the latest IGI2 updates, patches, and server events. Homecoming Gaming isn’t just a launcher — it’s a home for players who still love the original IGI2 spirit and want to see it evolve with new technology and fresh ideas.

Start your journey today — download HCLauncheR, connect to the world of IGI2 multiplayer, and experience the true essence of tactical action once again.
