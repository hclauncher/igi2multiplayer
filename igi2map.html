How to install Maps

All maps are provided by : HOMECOMING GAMING COMMUNITY

A Great Thanks to HOMECOMING MEMBERS

  • Firstly choose which maps do you want to download.
  • Then click the Download buttons and download your chosen maps to your PC.
  • Open them with WinRAR. If you don’t have WinRAR, to download Click Here and then install it first.
  • Extract the folders to the “multiplayer” folder inside the “MISSIONS” folder of the “pc” directory in your IGI-2 installation path.
    Example: C:\Program Files\IGI-2\pc\MISSIONS\multiplayer\
  • Once extracted, you will be able to play on servers running these maps or even host your own server using them.
