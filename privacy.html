Privacy Policy

Learn how Homecoming Gaming collects, uses, and protects your information while playing IGI-2 Multiplayer or using our community tools.

Last updated: October 30, 2025

1. Introduction

At Homecoming Gaming, accessible at https://igi2multiplayer.pages.dev, we respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy describes how we handle information when you use our website, tools, and multiplayer services, including HcLauncheR, HcCommanDeR, and HcBan.

2. Information We Collect

We collect limited personal information such as your name, in-game nickname, or contact details when you reach out through our Contact Page. We do not collect sensitive or financial information.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To respond to your support or contact requests.

  • To enhance community safety and prevent abuse or unfair gameplay.

4. Data Sharing and Security

We do not sell, rent, or trade personal data. Information is used solely within the Homecoming community for communication and server management. We apply standard security measures to safeguard all stored data from unauthorized access.

5. Cookies and YouTube Embeds

Some of our pages include embedded YouTube videos. YouTube may set cookies to optimize video playback or analytics. You can manage or disable cookies in your browser settings. Read our Cookie Policy for details.

6. Third-Party Links

Our site may link to external platforms (like YouTube, Discord, or Facebook). We are not responsible for their content or privacy policies. Please review their policies before sharing any data.

7. Children’s Privacy

We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 13. If you believe your child has shared information with us, please contact us via our Contact Page.

8. Policy Updates

This Privacy Policy may be updated periodically to reflect new tools, security standards, or regulations. The revised version will be posted here with an updated date.

9. Contact Us

If you have any questions or requests about your personal information, please contact us through our Contact Page.
