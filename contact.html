IGI-2 Multiplayer Support & Community Help

The Homecoming Gaming team is here to help all players of Project I.G.I-2: Covert Strike enjoy smooth multiplayer experiences. Whether you need assistance installing HCLauncheR, setting up HcCommanDeR, or learning how to host your own IGI2 multiplayer server, our community support members are available to guide you step by step.

If you’re facing connection issues, map loading problems, or version mismatches when joining servers, reach out to us directly through this contact page or join our Discord community. You can also email us anytime at igi2.homecoming@gmail.com for detailed support, feedback, or suggestions.

Our projects like HcBan and HcCommanDeR were built to make hosting and managing servers safe, easy, and reliable. HcBan automatically detects crashers and protects game servers from unwanted packets, while HcCommanDeR allows admins to monitor chat logs, player kills, and RCON commands in real time. These tools are part of the Homecoming Project — an effort to keep the IGI-2 legacy alive for players around the world.

If you’re new to IGI-2 multiplayer, our team can help you install and configure everything you need, from HCLauncheR setup to IP server listing and multiplayer troubleshooting. We also provide step-by-step guides on how to host IGI-2 servers within our launcher and ensure fair gameplay through regular updates and community moderation.

Homecoming Gaming is not just a project — it’s a community of players who believe in teamwork, fair play, and nostalgia. We aim to bring together both old and new players who want to relive classic tactical gameplay while discovering modern tools built by fans. Every suggestion we receive through this page helps us improve our tools and make the IGI-2 experience more stable and enjoyable.

Stay connected with us through Facebook, YouTube, Discord, or X (Twitter) for updates, announcements, and tutorials. We look forward to hearing from you — whether it’s a bug report, a new idea, or simply a “thank you” for keeping the IGI-2 Multiplayer world alive!
